Objectives

This course focuses on the foundational aspects of data literacy, which is defined as the ability to read, work with, analyze, and communicate with data. This course aims to help students understand and create data-driven questions and how to tell a convincing data story. Students will be able to interpret numeric summaries and visualizations of data. The course aids students in becoming critical consumers.
